Major Programs

Research Programs

SUPARCO conducts scientific research to support national space initiatives, including satellite development and remote sensing.

Satellite Navigation

Programs focused on satellite navigation for GPS-based services, geospatial applications, and enhancing national positioning systems.

Satellite Programs

Designing, building, and launching satellites to provide communication, remote sensing, and scientific data for Pakistan and regional applications.

Space Technology Applications

Leveraging space technology to improve agriculture, environment, urban planning, and disaster management through satellite data.

Products & Services

Aerial & Remote Sensing

Providing aerial and satellite-based remote sensing for mapping, monitoring, and advanced geospatial analysis.

Agriculture Monitoring

Monitoring crop health, soil conditions, and agricultural activities using satellite data for better yield management.

Remote Sensing Data

Collecting and providing satellite imagery and remote sensing data for scientific, commercial, and research purposes.

Space Weather

Monitoring solar activity and space weather phenomena to safeguard satellites, communications, and navigation systems.
